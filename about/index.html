about & contact

Hello! My name is Jane Zweibel. I am a Philadelphia based accessories designer with overflowing love for curly dogs, and making things. Little Bones was created in 2018 to showcase and share these affections.

I am a Colorado native with a BA in International Business from the American University in Paris and an MS in Fashion Design from Drexel University. While I am not at home with my dog Frances, I'm at work (with my dog Frances) in the Philadelphia Naval Yard at Anthropologie Home's headquarters where I am the Product Manager for Home Textiles.

I am an avid maker of many things including leathergoods, jewelry, ceramics, polymer clay figures, illustrations, stained glass, outerwear, evening wear, embroidery, complex excel spreadsheets-- and I illustrated, animated, and coded this entire website from scratch. The only thing I do not enjoy making is food!

Please visit my personal website (janemz.com) for more!
