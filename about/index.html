about & contact

Hello! My name is Jane Zweibel. I am a Philadelphia based accessories designer with overflowing love for curly dogs, and leathergoods. Little Bones was created in 2018 to showcase and share these affections.

I am a Colorado native, with a BA in International Business from the American University in Paris and an MS in Fashion Design from Drexel University. While I am not at home with my dog Frances, I'm at work (with my dog Frances) in the Philadelphia Naval Yard at Anthropologie Home's headquarters, where I am the Creative Operations Manager.

I am an avid maker of many things, including stained glass, outerwear, evening wear, and this website. Please visit my personal website (janemz.com) for more!
