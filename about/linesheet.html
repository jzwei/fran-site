Thanks for your interest in stocking Little Bones product! Email hello@little-bones.com for inquiries and include the name, address and website of your shop and a brief description of your store and product range.

Businesses are forbidden from selling Little Bones merchandise on any other online retail outlet (such as Amazon, Ebay, or Etsy).

Any changes or cancellation to orders must be emailed to hello@little-bones.com within 24 hours of submitting the order.

MINIMUM ORDER VALUE
First time wholesale buyers of Little Bones must meet the minimum order value of $500. Payment is due at the time of order. Reorders must meet a minimum order value of $300.

PRICING

Please allow at least 15 business days from the time of your order for completion of items. Larger orders may require more time; please contact hello@little-bones.com for information about availability.

SHIPPING
Shipping is not included in the cost of goods; buyer is accountable for shipping & handling fees. Shipping insurance must be requested to apply. Please allow up to 5 business days for order processing and fulfillment.

RETURNS & EXCHANGES
No returns of wholesale orders will be accepted unless items arrive broken or defective. Please inspect all products immediately upon receipt, and report any damages to hello@little-bones.com within 5 days for evaluation on a case-by-case basis. Little Bones reserves the right to refuse any claims submitted after 5 days.
