store policies

PRODUCT NOTES
Dimensions and colors may vary slightly due to the characteristics of handmade products, and natural materials. With use and exposure to the environment, the color of leather items will develop a deeper quality and a unique patina.

RETURNS & EXCHANGES
Due to the handmade nature of the product, returns are not generally accepted. However, if your item arrives damaged or defective, please contact hello@little-bones.com immediately for assistance.

Custom orders and monogrammed items are not returnable, exchangeable or refundable.

PAYMENT
Little Bones accepts all major credit cards, cash, checks, or PayPal transfers.

SHIPPING
Please allow up to 5 business days for order processing and fulfillment. We do not ship international orders at this time. Orders are shipped from Philadelphia, PA.
